Who we are?
Asia King Travel was founded in March 2007 . We specialise in personalised service and have deliberately remained specific in what we do rather than expand into a big outfit. We cater for a niche market while providing excellent value for money. Our head office includes management, sales, production and our marketing department in Hanoi. Our operations offices are throughout Vietnam, Laos and Cambodia. We offer tailor-made individual tours and private group tours, incentives tours as well as soft trekking and cycling tours. We have proven success over the past six years and are always striving to improve our service quality by creating new and interesting travel products, discovering new destinations and new activities to meet the expectations of customers and professional partners. Our customers include travellers and partners from France, USA, Australia and various European countries.
